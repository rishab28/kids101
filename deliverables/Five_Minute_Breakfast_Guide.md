# The 5-Minute Breakfast Guide
*Ultra-Fast Morning Recipes for Rushed School Days*

---

## The Morning Challenge
Between 6:00 AM and 7:00 AM, every second counts. This guide contains **8 recipes** that require zero fermenting, minimal chopping, and cook in under 5 minutes, ensuring your child gets a high-quality nutritional start without delaying the school bus.

---

### Recipe 1: Instant Oats-Banana Blender Crepes
*Mix, pour, flip, and serve in 4 minutes. High fiber and natural sweetness.*

*   **Prep Time:** 1 min | **Cook Time:** 3 mins | **Yield:** 3 crepes
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Rolled oats: 1/2 cup
    *   Ripe banana: 1/2
    *   Egg (optional) or yogurt: 2 tbsp
    *   Milk: 1/4 cup
    *   A2 Ghee: 1/2 tsp
*   **Instructions:**
    1.  **Blend:** Toss the oats, banana, yogurt, and milk into a blender. Blend on high speed for 30 seconds until a smooth batter forms.
    2.  **Pour:** Heat a non-stick pan, brush with ghee. Pour the batter and spread into thin circles.
    3.  **Cook:** Cook for 90 seconds on medium heat. Flip and cook for another 60 seconds.
    4.  **Roll:** Roll it up and pack. Tastes like a sweet banana wrap!

### Recipe 2: 3-Minute Paneer Bhurji Toast
*High protein breakfast that keeps them full and focused until lunchtime.*

*   **Prep Time:** 1 min | **Cook Time:** 2 mins | **Yield:** 1 serving
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Fresh paneer (crumbled): 1/2 cup
    *   Turmeric powder: A pinch
    *   Coriander powder: 1/4 tsp
    *   Salt: A pinch
    *   Butter: 1/2 tsp
    *   Whole wheat bread: 2 slices
*   **Instructions:**
    1.  **Toast:** Pop the bread slices into a toaster.
    2.  **Cook Bhurji:** In a pan, melt butter. Toss in crumbled paneer, turmeric, coriander, and salt. Stir continuously on high heat for 60-90 seconds. Do not overcook or the paneer will turn chewy.
    3.  **Assemble:** Pile the hot scrambled paneer on the toasted bread. Cut into triangles.

### Recipe 3: Speedy Rava (Semolina) Toast
*Crispy savory toast topped with yogurty veggies.*

*   **Prep Time:** 2 mins | **Cook Time:** 3 mins | **Yield:** 2 toasts
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Suji (Semolina): 3 tbsp
    *   Yogurt (Dahi): 2 tbsp
    *   Finely minced onion and capsicum: 2 tbsp
    *   Salt & pepper: A pinch
    *   Wheat bread: 2 slices
    *   Oil: 1/2 tsp
*   **Instructions:**
    1.  **Mix Batter:** Stir suji, yogurt, minced veggies, salt, and pepper in a bowl to form a thick paste.
    2.  **Spread:** Spread this mixture thickly on one side of each bread slice.
    3.  **Toast:** Heat a tawa, brush with oil. Place the bread mixture-side down. Cook for 2 minutes until golden brown and crispy. Flip and toast the other side for 1 minute.

### Recipe 4: Instant Besan Chilla (Chickpea Crepe)
*High-protein, gluten-free savory pancake.*

*   **Prep Time:** 1 min | **Cook Time:** 3 mins | **Yield:** 1 large chilla
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Besan (chickpea flour): 4 tbsp
    *   Carom seeds (Ajwain): A pinch
    *   Turmeric: A pinch
    *   Finely chopped coriander: 1 tsp
    *   Water: As needed
    *   Oil: 1/2 tsp
*   **Instructions:**
    1.  **Whisk:** Whisk besan, ajwain, turmeric, coriander, salt, and water in a mug to make a smooth pourable batter.
    2.  **Pan Fry:** Heat a tawa, brush with oil. Pour the batter and spread in a circular motion.
    3.  **Flip:** Cook on medium heat for 2 minutes, flip, and cook for 1 minute until golden.

### Recipe 5: Quick Apple-Almond Yogurt Cup
*No cooking required. Perfect for mornings when you wake up late.*

*   **Prep Time:** 2 mins | **Cook Time:** 0 mins | **Yield:** 1 serving
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Greek yogurt or hung curd: 1 cup
    *   Finely chopped sweet red apples: 1/2 cup
    *   Almond butter: 1 tbsp
    *   Honey: 1/2 tsp
*   **Instructions:**
    1.  **Assemble:** Layer yogurt in a cup.
    2.  **Top:** Add the sweet apple cubes, almond butter, and honey.
    3.  **Mix:** Let the child mix it up themselves for an interactive experience.

### Recipe 6: 2-Minute Mug Poha
*Slightly damp flattened rice prepared directly in a mug.*

*   **Prep Time:** 1 min | **Cook Time:** 2 mins | **Yield:** 1 serving
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Medium Poha (flattened rice): 1/2 cup
    *   Turmeric: A pinch
    *   Lemon juice: 1/2 tsp
    *   Peanuts (roasted): 1 tbsp
    *   Salt & sugar: A pinch
*   **Instructions:**
    1.  **Rinse:** Wash poha in a strainer under running tap water for 15 seconds. Let it drain.
    2.  **Season:** Toss poha into a microwave-safe mug or bowl. Stir in turmeric, salt, a pinch of sugar, and roasted peanuts.
    3.  **Microwave:** Cover with a small plate and microwave on high for 60 seconds.
    4.  **Finish:** Drizzle lemon juice and serve warm.

### Recipe 7: Express Mug Dhokla
*Spongy savory chickpea cake made in a microwave.*

*   **Prep Time:** 2 mins | **Cook Time:** 90 secs | **Yield:** 1 serving
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Besan: 4 tbsp
    *   Yogurt: 1 tbsp
    *   Lemon juice: 1/2 tsp
    *   Fruit salt (Eno): 1/4 tsp
    *   Water: 2 tbsp
    *   Oil: 1/2 tsp
*   **Instructions:**
    1.  **Mix:** Grease a microwave-safe mug with oil. Mix besan, dahi, lemon juice, water, and salt inside the mug.
    2.  **Activate:** Add fruit salt (Eno) and stir rapidly. The batter will foam and double in volume.
    3.  **Cook:** Microwave on high for 90 seconds. Let it rest for 30 seconds before cutting.

### Recipe 8: Green Peas Blender Hummus Toast
*Vibrant green high-fiber spread.*

*   **Prep Time:** 2 mins | **Cook Time:** 1 min | **Yield:** 2 toasts
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Boiled green peas: 1/2 cup
    *   Garlic: 1/2 small clove
    *   Olive oil: 1 tsp
    *   Lemon juice: 1/4 tsp
    *   Bread toasted: 2 slices
*   **Instructions:**
    1.  **Blend:** Throw boiled peas, garlic, olive oil, and lemon juice into a small jar blender. Blend for 20 seconds.
    2.  **Spread:** Spread this bright green protein hummus on toasted wheat bread. Top with thin paneer slices.
