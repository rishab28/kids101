# The 7-Day Breakfast & Tiffin Rotation Planner
*Eliminating Decision Fatigue & Morning Cooking Panic for Indian Moms*

---

## Why Rotational Planning Works
Most children become picky because they eat the same 3-4 "safe" items repeatedly, leading to sensory boredom and refusal of new foods. A rotational schedule prevents this by systematically introducing variety, while ensuring you know exactly what to cook before your alarm goes off.

This 7-day rotational planner is designed to balance proteins, complex carbs, and hidden vegetables using simple weekly prep cycles.

---

## Weekly Prep Cycle (Done on Sunday night)
Spend 20 minutes on Sunday to make morning cooking take under 10 minutes:
1.  **Chop & Freeze:** Grate carrots, chop spinach, boil peas, and store them in dry, airtight containers.
2.  **Batter Prep:** Make a batch of multi-grain cheela batter or soak dals. Store in the refrigerator.
3.  **Himalayan Fruit Bowl:** Wash, dry, and place fresh apples, pears, kiwis, and plums (sourced wax-free) in the fruit bowl.

---

## The 7-Day Rotational Schedule

### Day 1 (Monday): The Fresh Start
*   **Breakfast:** Oats-Banana Blender Pancakes (High fiber and sweet).
*   **School Tiffin:** Paneer & Carrot Stuffed Wheat Roll (wrapped in foil to keep soft).
*   **Fruit Snack:** Crisp sliced apples.

### Day 2 (Tuesday): The Veggie Stealth
*   **Breakfast:** Beetroot-tinted Pink Idli with Coconut Chutney.
*   **School Tiffin:** Moong Dal Cheela with hidden spinach puree.
*   **Fruit Snack:** Fresh sliced sweet pears.

### Day 3 (Wednesday): Mid-Week Crunch
*   **Breakfast:** Sooji-Curd Veggie Toast (toasted with a brush of A2 Ghee).
*   **School Tiffin:** Mini Vegetable Rava Uttapam (shaped like coins or stars).
*   **Fruit Snack:** Tangy kiwi slices.

### Day 4 (Thursday): High-Protein Fuel
*   **Breakfast:** Scrambled Paneer Bhurji with Whole Wheat Toast.
*   **School Tiffin:** Stuffed Gobhi-Paneer Paratha (kneaded with curd to keep soft for hours).
*   **Fruit Snack:** Sweet Hachiya persimmon slices or dried apricots.

### Day 5 (Friday): Fun Food Friday
*   **Breakfast:** Chocolate Ragi Porridge topped with chopped almonds.
*   **School Tiffin:** Healthy Veggie Oats Cutlets (baked or pan-seared).
*   **Fruit Snack:** Fresh sweet plums.

### Day 6 (Saturday): Weekend Brunch
*   **Breakfast:** Vegetable Poha with roasted peanuts and lemon squeeze.
*   **School Tiffin:** N/A (Weekend).
*   **Fruit Snack:** Fresh sliced apples.

### Day 7 (Sunday): Prep & Treat
*   **Breakfast:** Whole Wheat Vermicelli (Seviyan) Upma with lots of peas and carrots.
*   **School Tiffin:** N/A (Weekend).
*   **Fruit Snack:** Mixed dry fruits and dried plums.

---

> **KhaduFarm Quality Tip:** 
> When packing fresh fruits like apples or pears in the school tiffin, toss the slices in a teaspoon of fresh orange or lemon juice. The vitamin C prevents oxidation (browning), keeping the fruit looking fresh, crisp, and appealing to your child by lunchtime.
