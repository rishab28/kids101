# The 30-Day "Empty Tiffin" Calendar
*Daily Balanced Tiffin Combinations to Kill Morning Decision Fatigue*

---

## The Calendar Framework
This calendar is designed using a **Macro-Rotation System**. It ensures your child gets a varied combination of healthy fats, complex carbohydrates, and clean proteins while keeping morning prep time under 10 minutes. 

### Weekly Theme Guide:
*   **Mondays (Sweet Kickstart):** Keeps children excited for the school week using healthy, naturally sweetened breakfasts.
*   **Tuesdays (Rolls & Wraps):** Fun, finger-friendly food that is easy to eat on the go.
*   **Wednesdays (Mini-Shapes):** Bite-sized portions that look like a party in the box.
*   **Thursdays (Grains & Grits):** Slow-release complex carbs (oats, ragi, brown rice) to beat mid-week energy dips.
*   **Fridays (Fun Day Swaps):** Healthy recreations of street food/fast food (pasta, noodles, burger-style sliders).

## 3 Rules for Implementation:
1.  **Preparation Habit:** Keep your vegetable purees (spinach, beetroot, pumpkin) steamed, blended, and frozen in silicone ice cube trays on Sunday evenings. In the morning, simply pop out 1-2 cubes and melt them directly into your batter or sauces.
2.  **No Double Treats:** If the main tiffin is sweet (e.g. Day 1), always pair it with a fresh, acidic fruit (like apple slices) or savory roasted nuts. This prevents sugar overload.
3.  **The Friday Celebration:** Keep Friday fun. Present the pasta or pizza slide as a reward, letting them know that home-made versions are just as fun as cafe foods.
